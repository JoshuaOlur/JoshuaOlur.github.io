Fish Egg

Fish eggs are the initial stage of fish development, laid by female fish. They contain the genetic material necessary for the development of the embryo.

Fish Embryo

Fish embryos are the stage of development after fertilization of the egg. They undergo significant morphological and physiological changes before hatching.

Fish Larva

Fish larvae are the early developmental stage following hatching. They exhibit adaptations for dispersal and feeding before transitioning into juveniles.

Juvenile Fish

Juvenile fish are the young, developing stage of fish. They are smaller in size and may have different coloration compared to adults. Juvenile fish are typically learning to feed and navigate their environment.

Adult Fish

Adult fish are fully developed and sexually mature. They have reached their maximum size and exhibit behaviors related to reproduction, feeding, and habitat utilization.
